A BREATH OF FRESH AIR

Green Spaces Keep Us Sane

Like many millenials in my age group, I have a love for our national, state and county parks. Growing up in San Diego, I've been beyond blessed to have been surrounded by such beautiful scenaries. While the cost of living is astronomical, I firmly believe that we get what we pay for. While, I have been to breathtaking wonders, such as: Yosemite, Death Valley, Haleakala, Channel Islands National Park and other non-local nature reserves, I would like to showcase the beauty that is local to my area. Palomar Mountain State Park, Anza-Borrego State Park, Torrey Pines State Park, and other local gems don't often cross the minds of nature enthusiasts outside California, however, these natural preserves definitely deserve a mention.
